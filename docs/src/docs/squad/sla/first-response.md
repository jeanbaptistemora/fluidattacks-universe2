### Description

**90%** of comments and/or incidents
will have median first response time
of less than **12** office hours.

### Measurement

- Measured in
  calendar quarters.
- Taking into account
  all the groups
  of the organization
  over time.
- Taking into account
  all the historical data,
  not only that of
  the quarterly analisis period.
