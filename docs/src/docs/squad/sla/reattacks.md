### Description

**90%** of reattacks
to effectively closed vulnerabilities
will have a median response time
of less than **24** office hours.

### Measurement

- Measured in
  calendar quarters.
- Taking into account
  all the groups
  of the organization
  over time.
- Taking into account
  all the historical data,
  not only that of
  the quarterly analisis period.
