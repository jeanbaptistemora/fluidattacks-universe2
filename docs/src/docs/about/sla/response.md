### Criteria

- Applies only
  to plan Squad.
- It exists
  in both environment
  and source code.
- Coupled environment
  (exact deployment of the branch)
- Stable environment
  (**80%** of business days
  with no open eventualities)
- Complete dataset
  for the corresponding use case.
- Remote access with no human intervention
  (no captcha, OTP, etc.).

### Details

- Measured in
  calendar quarters.
- Taking into account
  all the groups
  of the organization
  over time.
- Taking into account
  all the historical data,
  not only that of
  the quarterly analisis period.
- Percentages are determined
  using percentiles.
- Office hours correspond to
  eight-hour bussiness days,
  like this:
  8AM-12M and 1PM-5PM.
- The reattacks to be considered
  will be those that had
  its vulnerability closed.
- The required transactionalities
  for measurement and penalty are:
    - Over **500** reattacks,
      comments 
      and/or incidents.
