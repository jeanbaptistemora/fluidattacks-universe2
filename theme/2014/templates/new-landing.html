How Continuous Hacking Works

  • Fixes

  • Facilitates the Manage of Vulnerabilities

  • Fixes is a platform that stores vulnerabilities and gives
    you access to their evidence in different phases and in
    real-time. You can easily and quickly classify and
    prioritize them, define their treatments, and check the
    status and progress of their remediation.

  • Skims

  • Automates the search of superficial
    vulnerabilities

  • Look for deterministic and peripheral vulnerabilities.
    Skims helps in the construction of exploits that break the
    build. It is executable in phases of pre-commit,
    CI time and post-deploy (CI compatible).
    Skims works for code (SAST), applications (DAST),
    infrastructure and cloud (AWS, Azure)
    and can be executed unlimited times.

  • Drills

  • Finds deep vulnerabilities

  • Drills, through human creativity and intelligence, finds
    deep and zero-day vulnerabilities during software
    development. Forget about false positives and get detailed
    evidence, accurate attack vectors, and log extraction.

  • Forces

  • Obliges the remediation of vulnerabilities

  • Forces, making use of human skills for the creation of
    exploits, breaks your build to force remediation of
    confirmed deep vulnerabilities, re-opened vulnerabilities,
    and new peripheral vulnerabilities.
    It can also verify your post-deploy production status.
